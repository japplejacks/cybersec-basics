# Best Free Firewall Software for Small Business: Complete Review of SonicWall TZ370 vs Fortinet FortiGate 40F for Under 50 Employees

When it comes to protecting your small business from cyber threats, choosing the right firewall is one of the most critical decisions you'll make. With cyberattacks targeting small businesses increasing by 37% in 2023, having robust network security isn't just recommended—it's essential for survival.

In this comprehensive review, we'll dive deep into two powerhouse contenders: the SonicWall TZ370 and the Fortinet FortiGate 40F. Both are designed specifically for small businesses with under 50 employees, but which one offers the best protection for your investment?

## Understanding Firewall Needs for Small Business

Before we jump into our head-to-head comparison, let's establish what small businesses really need from their firewall solution. Unlike enterprise environments with dedicated IT teams, small businesses require:

- **Easy setup and management**: Your firewall shouldn't require a cybersecurity degree to configure
- **Comprehensive threat protection**: Modern threats demand more than basic packet filtering
- **Scalability**: Your security solution should grow with your business
- **Cost-effectiveness**: Maximum protection without breaking the budget
- **Reliable support**: When issues arise, you need help fast

## SonicWall TZ370: The Veteran Defender

### Key Features and Specifications

The [SonicWall TZ370](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=SonicWall+TZ370&tag=applecore03-20) has built a solid reputation in the small business market. Here's what makes it stand out:

**Performance Specifications:**
- Firewall throughput: 1.5 Gbps
- VPN throughput: 200 Mbps
- Maximum concurrent connections: 150,000
- Supported users: Up to 50
- Ports: 5 x 1GbE interfaces

**Security Features:**
- Deep Packet Inspection (DPI)
- Intrusion Prevention System (IPS)
- Gateway Anti-Virus and Anti-Spyware
- Content and URL filtering
- Application control and bandwidth management
- SSL/TLS decryption and inspection

### Strengths of the SonicWall TZ370

**User-Friendly Interface**: SonicWall's SonicOS interface is intuitive, making it accessible for small business owners without extensive IT backgrounds. The setup wizard guides you through initial configuration, and the dashboard provides clear visibility into network activity.

**Comprehensive Security Suite**: The TZ370 comes with SonicWall's Gateway Anti-Virus, Anti-Spyware, and Intrusion Prevention services included for the first year. This integrated approach means you're not juggling multiple security vendors.

**Proven Track Record**: SonicWall has been protecting small businesses for over two decades. Their threat intelligence network processes over 1.2 million malware samples daily, ensuring your firewall stays updated against the latest threats.

### Potential Drawbacks

**Subscription Costs**: While the hardware is reasonably priced, ongoing subscription costs for security services can add up over time. After the first year, you'll need to budget for annual renewals.

**Performance Under Load**: Some users report slowdowns when all security features are enabled simultaneously, particularly SSL inspection on high-traffic networks.

## Fortinet FortiGate 40F: The Performance Champion

### Key Features and Specifications

The [Fortinet FortiGate 40F](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=Fortinet+FortiGate+40F&tag=applecore03-20) represents Fortinet's entry-level offering, but don't let that fool you—it packs serious security capabilities:

**Performance Specifications:**
- Firewall throughput: 10 Gbps
- Threat Protection throughput: 600 Mbps
- VPN throughput: 1.8 Gbps
- Maximum concurrent sessions: 200,000
- Supported users: Up to 80
- Ports: 2 x 1GbE WAN, 14 x 1GbE switch ports

**Security Features:**
- Next-Generation Firewall (NGFW) capabilities
- Advanced Threat Protection (ATP)
- Web filtering and application control
- Intrusion Prevention System (IPS)
- Anti-malware and sandboxing
- SD-WAN capabilities
- Built-in wireless controller

### Strengths of the FortiGate 40F

**Superior Performance**: The FortiGate 40F significantly outperforms the SonicWall in raw throughput numbers. This performance advantage becomes crucial as your business grows and network demands increase.

**Integrated Approach**: Fortinet's Security Fabric provides unified management across all security components. The 40F can serve as your firewall, switch, wireless controller, and SD-WAN hub—all in one device.

**Advanced Threat Intelligence**: FortiGuard Labs processes over 100 billion security events daily, feeding real-time threat intelligence to your firewall. This massive data collection provides exceptional protection against zero-day threats.

### Potential Challenges

**Learning Curve**: FortiOS is powerful but complex. Small businesses without dedicated IT staff may find the interface overwhelming initially.

**Feature Complexity**: The 40F offers extensive capabilities, but accessing advanced features may require additional training or professional assistance.

## Head-to-Head Comparison

### Performance and Throughput

**Winner: FortiGate 40F**

The FortiGate 40F crushes the SonicWall TZ370 in performance metrics. With 10 Gbps firewall throughput versus 1.5 Gbps, and 1.8 Gbps VPN throughput versus 200 Mbps, the FortiGate provides significantly more headroom for growth.

### Ease of Use and Management

**Winner: SonicWall TZ370**

SonicWall's interface is more intuitive for non-technical users. The setup process is streamlined, and day-to-day management tasks are straightforward. FortiOS offers more capabilities but requires more technical expertise to fully utilize.

### Security Effectiveness

**Winner: Tie (with slight edge to FortiGate)**

Both firewalls provide excellent security protection. FortiGate's threat intelligence network is larger, and their custom ASIC chips provide hardware-accelerated security processing. However, SonicWall's deep packet inspection engine is also highly effective.

### Value and Total Cost of Ownership

**Winner: Depends on your needs**

The [SonicWall TZ370](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=SonicWall+TZ370+firewall&tag=applecore03-20) typically has a lower upfront cost but ongoing subscription fees. The FortiGate 40F may cost more initially but often provides better long-term value due to its integrated features and superior performance.
