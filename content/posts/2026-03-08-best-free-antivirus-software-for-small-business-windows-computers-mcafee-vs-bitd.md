# Best Free Antivirus Software for Small Business Windows Computers: McAfee vs Bitdefender vs Kaspersky 2024 Comparison

## Why Small Businesses Need Dedicated Antivirus Protection

Small businesses face unique cybersecurity challenges. Unlike large enterprises with dedicated IT security teams, small businesses often operate with limited resources and expertise. This makes them attractive targets for cybercriminals who assume they have weaker defenses.

Modern threats go far beyond traditional viruses. Today's small businesses must defend against:

- **Ransomware attacks** that can encrypt critical business data
- **Phishing emails** targeting employee credentials
- **Advanced persistent threats (APTs)** that can remain undetected for months
- **Zero-day exploits** that target newly discovered vulnerabilities
- **Business email compromise (BEC)** schemes

While Windows Defender provides basic protection, dedicated antivirus solutions offer enhanced detection capabilities, advanced threat intelligence, and business-focused features that can make the difference between a minor security incident and a business-ending disaster.
